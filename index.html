Not Deep Learning. Smart Learning.

PULSE is inspired by nature. Your brain doesn't fire all neurons at once; neither do our models. Here are the 3 pillars of our architecture.

No Hidden Layers

Instead of the classic "Input - Hidden - Output" stack, we use a free-form biological Mesh structure. Data flows organically through the network.

Unstructured Mesh
CORE TECH

Sparse Activation

We only simulate neurons that are actively firing. This reduces the computational load by up to 90%, bypassing OOM errors.

Only firing neurons processed

Hebbian & Hybrid

The Hebbian rule is split: Serial logic operations are handled by the CPU, while parallel weight updates are offloaded to the GPU.
